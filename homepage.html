Welcome to MailingAPI!

We provide free SMTP SSL E-Mail sending using our API

Why use MailingAPI?

We offer simple SMTP E-Mail sending without asking for registration or any kind of your data.
We do not store any data you send to us in logs or anything else. Once you have the answer of our server to your request, our server immediately forgets who you are.
Since we use SMTP protocol with SSL (port 465), our wervice can be used for a wide range of E-Mail providers.

Please take into consideration that, since our service is completely free, our service falls into some kind of "sleep mode" after some time of inactivity. If you send a request to our server when it is sleeping, it may take a few seconds longer than usual for a request to be processed.


How the JSON object has to look like for '/api/sendmail':

{"smtp_server":SERVER-ADDR,"from_email":SENDER,"from_password":SENDER-PWD,"to_email":RECIEVER,"subject":SUBJECT,"content":MESSAGE}
What do the parameters mean?

  • SERVER-ADDR defines the SMTP Server domain of your provider
  • SENDER Your E-Mail address
  • SENDER-PWD Your password for your provider. We do not store this longer than your request takes.
  • RECIEVER is the E-Mail address of the person who should recieve the E-Mail. We do only support sending E-Mails to one person at a time, so this need to be a String.
  • SUBJECT The sentence the reciever will see the first when he looks at the E-Mail
  • MESSAGE is the text you want to send. This can yeither be normal text OR HTML formatted text.
